01

BOOK COVER DESIGN

This project was completed for a project in my high school media arts class. The objective was to pick a genre and using photoshop create a book cover. All photos used are royalty free, and the design reflects my chosen genre, which was thriller. The process involved selecting and editing images to align with the theme, adding typography that complements the mood, and experimenting with colour grading and composition to achieve a professional-looking final product. Through this project, I developed a stronger understanding of visual storytelling and learned how to effectively use Photoshop tools such as layer masks, blending modes, and custom textures.

04

PERSONAL POSTERS

This project was completed as part of GBDA 202, with the goal of creating a digital self-portrait as a designer through the medium of posters. I created four unique posters, each highlighting a different aspect of my identity as a designer: my skills, my favourite designer, my goals, and my design principles. The overarching objective was to communicate my story and style as a designer. I used Photoshop as my primary tool, allowing me to experiment with layering, textures, and effects to bring my vision to life. This project not only showcased my design abilities but also gave me the opportunity to reflect on my growth and aspirations in the field.
