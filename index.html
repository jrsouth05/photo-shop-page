01

BOOK COVER DESIGN

This project was completed as part of a media arts class assignment in high school. The objective was to choose a specific genre and, using Photoshop, design a compelling book cover. I selected the thriller genre and focused on creating a design that would capture its suspenseful and gripping nature. All the images used in the project were royalty-free, ensuring the ethical use of resources. The process began with carefully selecting and editing images to align with the theme, followed by incorporating typography that enhanced the mood and reinforced the genre's aesthetic. I explored various design elements, including experimenting with colour grading, fine-tuning the composition for balance, and adding details to create a professional and polished final look. Key Photoshop tools such as layer masks, blending modes, and custom textures were used to bring my vision to life. This project not only strengthened my understanding of visual storytelling but also helped me improve my technical skills in Photoshop, offering valuable insights into how design choices can effectively communicate a narrative.

PRODUCT POSTER DESIGN

This project was completed as part of my Communications
Technology course, where the primary goal was to select a
product and design a visually compelling advertisement for
it using Photoshop.The objective was not only to effectively
present the product but also to demonstrate a thorough
understanding of Photoshop techniques. This included applying
concepts such as layout design, colour theory, and typography
to ensure the ad was both visually appealing. We were encouraged
to experiment with tools like layer effects, blending modes, and
image retouching to enhance the overall composition and
create a polished, professional-quality final piece.
